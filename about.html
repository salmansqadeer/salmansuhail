About
Equal parts introverted and extroverted. Love to be outdoors, aspire to read more, and not easily offended.

By day I am a member of the product leadership team at Opower, responsible for our customer engagement vertical of products. At Opower, we use the power of computer science, data science, and behavioral science to motivate people across the globe to save more energy (resulting in 11 Billing kWh's of energy saved, 12 Billion lbs of CO2 not released into the atmosphere, and over a billion dollars in end user savings). In my spare time I love to be creating new things through code, music, or art, exploring the outdoors by foot or by bike, finding new shortcuts (or long cuts) to get to work, and occasionally washing the dishes.

I consider myself to be a citizen of the world, obsessed with design bordering on art, a developer, a designer, a product manager, a maker & builder, a husband. I specialize in facilitating the product design and development lifecycle from ideation, implementation, marketing to product launch and growth. I pride myself on living at the intersection of technology, design, and business, and nothing makes me happier than working with amazing teams and building products that impact the lives of thousands of people.

I am currently based out of San Francisco, but constantly imagine where life may take me next.
